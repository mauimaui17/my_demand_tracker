Hello, world!

This app displays demand information for elective subjects. Currently displaying electives offered by CAS and CAFS only.

To add to your wishlist, simply sign-up and provide your information, preferably using your UP email, and head on over to the Course Catalog.

Some of your data will be used for in-depth demand analysis, specifically your:

  • Batch
  • Course
  • Degree Program

You may use the site without signing up, but this prototype is intended to be fully crowdsourced information, so every bit helps.
